How many members per team?

+

Each team must have exactly 3 members. All team members should bring their own laptops as workstations are not provided.

What should we bring?

+

Each team must bring their own laptops (mandatory), extension cords for power, valid college ID, and most importantly - your problem-solving skills and enthusiasm!

What skills do I need?

+

Basic programming knowledge is helpful but not mandatory. The challenges test logical thinking, pattern recognition, cryptography basics, and teamwork. All skill levels are welcome!

When and where is the event?

+

The event will be held on campus at SIT Nagpur. Exact dates will be announced soon. Follow our social media for updates!

How do I register?

+

Click the "REGISTER NOW" button at the top of the page to register your team. You'll need team name, member details, and contact information. Registration is free!

How long is the event?

+

The event is a full-day competition with five rounds. Each round has specific time limits. Plan to be available for the entire day.

Are there any prerequisites?

+

You must be a college student with a valid student ID. Basic computer literacy and problem-solving skills are recommended. No specific programming language expertise is required.

Can I change my team after registration?

+

Team changes are allowed up to 48 hours before the event. Contact us immediately if you need to make changes. Last-minute changes may not be accommodated.

Will food be provided?

+

Yes! Refreshments and meals will be provided during the event. Please inform us of any dietary restrictions during registration.
